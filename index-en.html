How to File a Claim

Simple steps to receive your reimbursement

1

Claim Notification

Contact Jasindo Call Center at 1500073 or visit https://mkp.ppibroker.com within max. 45 calendar days of the incident. Emergency: (+62) 818 343 494 (WhatsApp PPIB).

2

Document Submission

Claim form, copy of policy & ID (KTP/Passport), tickets & boarding passes, official authority report, medical summary (if applicable).

3

Verification Process

Jasindo's claims team verifies all documents. FCR: 1 working day; Non-FCR: max. 10 working days after complete documents are received.

4

Claim Payment

Upon approval, payment is transferred within 30 calendar days. Accepted claims release the Insurer from all obligations for the benefit concerned.

Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ — Common Questions About the Product

04
01

What is Jasindo Travel Insurance?

Jasindo Travel Insurance is an additional protection that provides accident coverage, 100% ticket refund, and loss of belongings during your journey.

02

How is it different from mandatory Jasa Raharja insurance?

Jasa Raharja's mandatory insurance only applies if a vessel accident occurs, whereas Jasindo Travel Insurance does not require a vessel accident to have happened.

03

How much is the premium and does it apply per person?

The premium is charged per passenger and will be displayed during the ticket purchase process. The premium amount follows the applicable terms at the time of transaction.

04

When does coverage start and end?

Coverage applies as per the travel date stated on the ticket and policy, with a coverage period of up to 1×24 hours as per product terms.

05

What types of accidents are covered?

Accidents that occur suddenly, unintentionally, resulting in injury, permanent disability, or death as per policy terms.

06

Does coverage only apply while on board the vessel?

Coverage also applies during the journey and wherever you are, as long as it is within the policy period — 1 day on the date of departure.

07

Can sudden illness before departure be claimed?

Reimbursement for travel tickets can be claimed. However, treatment costs other than accidents and outside the departure date are not covered.

08

What is the maximum benefit I can receive?

The maximum benefit is as stated in the benefit table; however, the amount paid is usually in accordance with the ticket price/receipt.

09

How do I file a claim?

Visit the claims portal at mkp.ppibroker.com, log in using your phone number and OTP. Details are also sent to your email after purchasing the policy.

10

What items are covered in case of loss?

Electronics, bags, and watches, subject to the limits and conditions stated in the policy or product summary (RIPLAY).

11

What documents are required to file a claim?

Personal ID, travel ticket, insurance brochure, and supporting documents according to the claim type. Details are communicated via the claims portal.

12

How long does the claim process take?

Estimated completion of maximum 7 working days after documents are declared complete and compliant.

13

Who do I contact to file a claim or get assistance?

WhatsApp PPIB: (+62) 818 343 494 · Call Center: 1500073 · Portal: mkp.ppibroker.com

14

Will I receive my policy document after purchase?

Yes, you can view and download your policy document at mkp.ppibroker.com by logging in with your phone number and OTP.

15

How do I view or download my policy?

Log in at mkp.ppibroker.com using your phone number and OTP, then select the policy card you wish to download.

16

What if passenger or manifest data is incorrect?

Immediately contact WhatsApp PPIB (+62) 818 343 494 with your transaction data and ID.

17

My registered phone number is lost or inactive?

Immediately contact WhatsApp PPIB (+62) 818 343 494 to update your data, providing your transaction data and ID.

18

Why was my claim rejected or payment not received?

Claims may be rejected if they do not meet policy terms, including risks that are not covered or incomplete documentation.

19

Is this product officially regulated by OJK?

This product is issued by PT Asuransi Jasa Indonesia, a member of IFG, under the supervision of the Financial Services Authority (OJK).
